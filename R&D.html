Research & Development is the core strength of Spectrum. It performs many functions to be abreast with the latest innovations in the industry and contributing some of its own. R&D at Spectrum is manned by a team of highly qualified experts and backed by a strong setup of instruments and lab scale plants.

  • Pilot Plants for Intermediate synthesis and Solvent Dyes
  • All Pilot Plants are equipped with automated heating & cooling systems
